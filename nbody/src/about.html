Quick help:

  • add planets by double clicking; then click to place a velocity vector
  • modify planet properties at any time from the side panel (try it!)
  • remove planets by clicking "remove planet"
  • move view around by dragging background
  • zoom by using scrollwheel (zooms where you point a la Google Maps)
  • the direction is to the east when the slider is at zero; it increases counter-clockwise
  • you can set the default mass for new planets by setting the mass in the mass text box and pressing return

Known issues:

  • numerical integration is (by definition) inaccurate, and if planets experience extreme accelerations, they escape
  • direction as a slider from 0 to 2*pi is unintuitive; however, I wanted to also show the linear velocity and couldn't come up with a better arrangement
  • the app isn't very usable at small resolutions since the side panel takes some space
